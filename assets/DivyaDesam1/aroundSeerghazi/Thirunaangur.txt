<h1>Sri Purushothama Perumal Temple, Seergazhi</h1>
<h2>About the Temple &amp; Location:</h2>
<p>This  temple lies in Thirunaangur of Tanjore district in Tamil Nadu. It’s  about 5 miles away from Seerkazhi. Bus facilities are also available.</p>
<p><b>Sthala Puranam:</b></p>
<p>As  the perumal of this place gave the Thirupaarkadal and stands as Rama  the greatest among the men so named as Purushothaman along with his wife  Purushothama Naayaki - Periya piratti, this place is called as Thiru  Vann Purushothamam.</p>
<p>As Lord Narayana gave generously the Thirupaarkadal to Upamanyu Maharishi, the theertham is known as Thirupaarkadal Theertham.</p>
<p>Sanjeevi  Mooligai (An herb) saved Lord Rama itself. So in order to indicate  this, the Vimaanam of this place is named as Sanjeevi Vigraha Vimaanam.</p>
<p>The  term Purushothamam means the person who is best among Bhaktar, Muktar,  Nitheyar and Purushar (ie) among Devotees, the person who had attained  eternity, the soul of the human being always lives with the god.</p>

<p>The  Utsavar Purushotaman found in this sthalam is said to be so beautiful  and can easily attract everyone's attention and heart towards him.</p>

<p><b>Specials:</b></p>
<p>1. The day after every Thai Amavasai, Garuda Sevai is celebrated excellently here.</p>
<p><b>Moolavar:</b></p>
<p>The  Moolavar of this Divyadesam is Sri Purushotaman. The Moolavar is in the  Nindra (Standing) Thirukkolam facing his thirumugham towards east  direction. Prathyaksham for Upamanyu Maharishi.</p>
<p><b>Thayaar:</b></p>

<p>The Thayaar found in this sthalam is Purushotama Naayaki.</p>
<p><b>Utsavar:</b></p>
<p>The Utsavar found in this sthalam is also Purushotaman.</p>
<p><b>Mangalasasanam:</b></p>

Total - 10 Paasurams.</p>

Thirupaarkadal Theertham.</p>

Sanjeevi Vigraha Vimaanam.</p>
