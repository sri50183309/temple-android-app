<h1>Sri Thamaraiyal Kelvan Perumal Temple, Thiru Paarthanpalli - Seergazhi</h1>
<h2>About the Temple &amp; Location:</h2>
<p>This  temple lies near Thiruvennkadu of Tanjore district in Tamil Nadu. It's  about 7 miles away from Seerkazhi and 2 miles away from Thiruvennkadu.</p>
<p><b>Sthala Puranam:</b></p>
<p>Lord Varuna is the Lord of Rain. He once stole the wife of Bhruhaspathi the Devaguru and was caught by the Brahma Hathi dhosam.</p>
<p>As  Lord Shiva had the dharsan of Lord Narayana at the 11 Thirunangur  Kshetrams after getting releived from Brahma Hati dhosam, Lord Varuna  too came here and got relief from his dhosam.</p>
<p>As Arjuna - Parthan got dharsan of Lord Narayana here, the Perumal has got the name Parthasarathy.</p>
<p>Moolavar  and Utsavar are found along with Periya Pirati, Bhoomi piratti and  Neeladevi as like Lord Narayana, the Vimaanam got its name as Narayana  Vimaanam.</p>

<p>As  the Ekadasa Rudhiran got dharsan here, the Pushkarani is known as  shankarasaras where Shankara means Lord Shiva and as an honour to Ganges  which is in the Top of Lord Shiva's head, it is also known as Ganga  Theertham.</p>

<p>In  this temple itself there is a utsavar idol of Kolavalli Raman who has  (shell) Shangu, (wheel) Chakram, (a weapon) Ghadai, (bow) Vill and  (Arrow) Ambu inhis hands. The Moolavar of this temple is found in a  Tamarind Orcherd near the temple.</p>
<p>It is said that meaning of the Sharma sloa, the Gota sloka ("Sharva dharman.") was got at this place only.</p>
<p>Moolavar:</p>
<p>The  Moolavar of this Divyadesam is Sri Thamaraiyal Kelvan. Also known as  Lakshmi Rangar. He is giving his seva Nindra (Standing) Thirukkolam  facing his thirumugham towards east direction. Prathyaksham for Parthan,  Varunan and Ekadasa Rudhirar.</p>
<p><b>Thayaar:</b></p>
<p>The Thayaar found in this sthalam is Thamarai Nayaki.</p>
<p><b>Utsavar:</b></p>

<p>The Utsavar found in this sthalam is Parthasarathy.</p>
<p><b>Utsavam:</b></p>
<p>Every year during the Tamil Month Thai, on the Pushya Nakshatram day, a utsavam of the pushkarani is celebrated.</p>
